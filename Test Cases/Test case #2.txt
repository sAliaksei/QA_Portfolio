Title:
Putting together an unfinished puzzle

Preconditions:
There is an unfinished puzzle

Steps:
1. Tap an unfinished puzzle.
2. Tap the "Continue" button.
3. Put the puzzle together.
4. Tap the "More puzzles" button.

Expected result:
1. The pop-up window appears with the following text "The unfinished puzzle will be lost if you start a new game".
2. The puzzle collection menu appears after the advertisement is played. Pieces of the puzzle are located where they were placed by the user.
3. The pop-up window "Well done!" appears.
4. Main menu appears after watching advertisements. Progress of the completed puzzle is 100%. The user received the number of coins according to the conditions.
